Objetive

Design a catalog system for hiring and management of professionals, to facilitate the hiring of these by customers, in a practical and simple way in which both the professional and the client can feel comfortable when communicating the processes that are carried out. This will be achieved by means of research on the part of each of the members of the team, as well as putting into practice the knowledge imparted.

Mision

Our mission is to connect professionals with potential clients, facilitating the time needed to get projects off the ground. In turn, facilitating interfaces for planning, verification and remuneration.


Vision

To become the best and most recognized option for finding professionals to be hired on a temporary basis, boosting their reputation, as well as ensuring the success of the projects entrusted to them.
